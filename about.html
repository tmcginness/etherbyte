About EtherByte

EtherByte was founded with the idea that in order for cryptocurrencies to evolve from their current status they needed real-world application. Working with money processors to allow marketplaces to take advantage of this growing asset class we plan to deploy EtherByte to a wide variety of online marketplaces. Our currency is currently designed on the two decimal system that allows for easy conversion with our retail clients.

At the forefront of our design, we will be utilizing our lighting fast transaction times and the security of the Blockchain to establish a payment system that allows the transfer of EtherByte to vendors effortlessly. We understand the desire for more decentralized payment systems that allow greater anonymity and security when using cryptocurrencies. For this asset class to grow it is important to be using and exchanging them for real-world goods.

We hope that those who invest along with us share this dream and will work alongside us to achieve not only these small goals but, the future goals of this company and the ascension of the blockchain from simple code into practical and daily use.
